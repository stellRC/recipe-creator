Empanadas

Get ready for a cooking adventure!

Crust

  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Cubbed Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Egg
  1. Stir four and salt before mixing in butter
  2. Whisk egg with water
  3. Add egg to flour, add more water
  4. Form dough into ball, flatten into disk
  5. Wrap tightly in plastic and refrigerate
  6. Remove Dough and cut into 10-16 pieces
  7. Roll each piece into a circle

Filling

  • 1 Russet Potato
  • 1 Golden Potato
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Chopped Red Onion
  • 1/2 Red Bellpepper
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Tsp Cinammon
  • 3/4 Cup Broth
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Cardamon
  1. Boil potatoes with Salt
  2. Sautee veggies & spices in butter
  3. Add potatoes and let simmer until broth disapears
  4. Spoon topping onto dough circles then fold together
  5. Brush egg wash over surface
  6. Bake & serve hot

Sauce

  • 1 Cup Parsely
  • 1/2 Cup Cilantro
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/3 Cup Olive Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Lime Juice
  • 2 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt & Pepper
  1. Mix!

Tips

  • Refrigerate for 1 - 1.5 hours
  • Don't spoon too much topping onto dough
  • Check at empanadas at 27 minutes
